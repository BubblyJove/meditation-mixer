Initial release of Meditation Mixer

Features:
• Tone generator with frequency presets (Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta)
• Audio layer mixing with volume controls
• Import your own audio files
• Built-in ambient sounds
• Session timer with fade-out
• Preset save/load
• Streak tracking and achievements
• Neumorphic dark UI
• Background playback with notification controls
• Completely offline, no tracking
